2014 Region 5 Textbook Fair


Region 5 ESC welcomes you to the 2014 Textbook Fair for Science, Math (K-8), and Technology Applications (2014-15 adoption).

The Textbook Fair will be held Tuesday, January 14, 2014 on the 4th floor at Edison Plaza, 350 Pine Street, Beaumont, Texas from 8:30- 4:00 P.M.

Admission is free and open to any school district personnel.

There is no registration but attendants will need to pick up a badge when they enter the building.

Textbook publishers will be holding break-out sessions to showcase their offerings for all subjects.

The publishers will have booths outside of the presentation rooms where you may speak with the textbook representatives personally and gather information.
